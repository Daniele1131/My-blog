April 13: Champions league update

April 13

PSG eliminates Bayern Munich from the champions league

In very well faught game filled with plenty of action and scoring chances, Bayern Munich
managed to win the game 1-0. Unfortunately, this result wans't enough for the champions to
qualify to the next round, since PSG scored more away goals (3) during the first leg. PSG are
now getting ready to face Manchester City in the semi- final of this prestigeous competition.

Chelsea eliminates Porto from the champions league semi-final

As everyone predicted, Chelsea eliminated Porto FC from the champions league semi-final
in a 2-1 aggregate score over both the home and away games. Chelsea played two very good
games, dominating possession ad shots on goal. As well nearly keeping Porto off the score sheet
over that span. They now prepare to play the very dangerous Real Madrdid in the champions league of semi-final.
