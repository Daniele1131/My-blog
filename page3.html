April 14: Champions league update 2.0

April 14

Real Madrdid eliminates Liverpool from the champions league

Real Madrid knocked out Liverpool from the champions league today asserting their domminance throughout this competition. The score finished 3-1 on aggregate for the spanish club. Although Liverpool played a decent game, Madrid did an excellent job of finding gaps between the defence and capitalizing on their scoring chances. The whites are currently in preperation mode for their upcoming semi-final with Chelsea.

Manchester City eliminates Borussia Dortmund from the champions league

The number 1 team in England Manchester City managed to eliminate the german power house Borussia Dortmund in a very well played champions league quarter final with a 4-2 win on aggregate score. With this win, City moves on to play PSG in a semi-final for the ages. Most experts predict that the winner of this semi-final will most likely move on to win the tournament. We will anxiousley be waiting for this epic match-up.
