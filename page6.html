April 17

Montreal Football Club great season debut

Today, the Montreal Impact kick-started their 2021 season with a very reassuring 4-2 win over their fierce rivals Toronto FC. Montreal played a very composed and fast-paced game and took advantage of the many scoring opportunities they had. This performance is a great bounce back from the poor 2020 campaign they had last year. It is safe to say that the administration has done an excellent job of acquiring excellent players over the span of this off-season. If this is an indicator of how they will be playing this year, we are most certainly in for a treat!
