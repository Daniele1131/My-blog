April 15

A soon to be"reasuring" end to the Premier League season

Today, the Premier League announced that they will re-arrange the fixture in order for each club to play a home match in front of fans. This is excellent news since these players have gone nearly over a year without a single fan at their games. This will surely boost morale and motivation around the league which is always a good thing.
