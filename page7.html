The creation of a Europeen Super League threatning to ruin soccer's future

On April 18th, 2021, the creation of a new European "Super League" was announced. The idea was for the top European football clubs to break away from UEFA and start their own competition in 2021. Twelve different teams from three different leagues decided to join this new league. Liverpool, Chelsea, Arsenal, Tottenham, Manchester United, Manchester City, Barcelona, Real Madrid, Athletico Madrid, Juventus, AC Milan and Inter Milan were the twelve teams who accepted the invitation to this new competition.
